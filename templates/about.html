Our mission is to empower farmers, traders, consumers, and policymakers by providing real-time insights into food prices and availability across Nigeria’s regions. Whether you’re planning your next market trip, optimizing supply chains, or shaping food policies, we’re here to help you make smarter decisions with transparent, accessible data.

Food Price Tracker connects communities by bridging information gaps, ensuring food reaches where it’s needed most, and fostering a fairer, more efficient food distribution system.
